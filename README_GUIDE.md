# Stardust Guide AI - Vault Guardian & Architect

![Stardust Logo](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/stardust-vault/stardust_agreements/main/stardustlogo.png) (Placeholder URL or local image if available)

The **Stardust Guide** is a premium, logic-driven AI assistant designed for the Stardust Vault platform. It helps users secure their digital legacy through intelligent automation, security auditing, and personalized succession planning.

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🛡️ Guardian Mode (Security Audit)
The Guide performs deep-sector security scans of your vault to identify vulnerabilities, missing nominees, or unprotected asset categories. It provides a real-time **Security Integrity Score** based on your preparedness.

### 📐 Vault Architect (Planning)
A personalized planning engine that interviews you about your life situation (family, assets, legal status) to generate a **Security Roadmap**. It gives specific pointers on how to protect your future.

### 🔄 Succession Protocol (Dead-Man Switch)
The Guide facilitates the setup of "Inactivity Triggers" that automate the transfer of your most sensitive data to your chosen guardians when conditions are met.

### 🔐 Zero-Knowledge Interaction
All AI interactions are processed with a focus on privacy. The Guide explains and implements **AES-256-GCM** encryption standards to ensure your data is always secure.

## 🛠️ Technology
- **Frontend**: HTML5, Vanilla JavaScript, CSS3 (Glassmorphism & Deep Space Theme)
- **Logic**: Custom Intent-Scoring Engine with Noisy Text Handling
- **Aesthetics**: Outfit & Space Mono typography with dynamic SVG animations
